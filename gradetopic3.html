LESSONS

FRACTIONS

A fraction represents a part of a whole. The top number, called the numerator, shows how many parts we have, while the bottom number, the denominator, shows how many equal parts the whole is divided into. For example, 1/3 means one out of three equal parts. Comparing fractions like 1/3 and 1/5 helps us see which part is larger.



DIVISION

Division is the process of splitting a number into equal parts. For example, if you have 20 candies and want to share them equally among 4 friends, each friend gets 5 candies because 20 รท 4 = 5. Division helps solve problems related to sharing, grouping, or breaking things into smaller parts..



GEOMETRY

Geometry is the study of shapes, sizes, and positions. A rectangle is a four-sided shape. Understanding geometry helps us recognize shapes in everyday objects like a traffic signs.
